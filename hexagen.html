At the moment, you can only use images which have the same height as width. E.g. use an image with 500px * 500px, or 1000px * 1000px ... This will be fixed soon!

How to?

Fold it.

  • Have a look at this excellent hexaflexagon template with instructions on how to fold it.

Print it to a real paper.

  • Right-click on the large image at th bottom and click view image or however it is called in your browser.
  • Copy the image, again with a right-click.
  • Paste it into a Word/OpenOffice document and arrange it such that the image filles the long edge of the paper. Leave some room at the borders because printers usually can't print borderless.

Change the images.

  • Double click on the preview images to load a file.
  • or simply drag and drop them.
  • Single click on a preview image to change the orientation of the hexagon extracted.
